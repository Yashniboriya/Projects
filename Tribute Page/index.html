Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand) born 7 July 1981), commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017.MS Dhoni or Mahendra Singh Dhoni or a Lieutenant colonel, Dhoni is a name of a legend and only captain who won ICC Trophy in all formats. He is the best 'finisher' in limited-overs formats also he is the fastest.
Dhoni was the highest paid cricketer in the IPL auction in 2008. Dhoni has started playing for CSK (Chennai Super Kings). Under Dhoni's leadership, Chennai Super Kings have won the IPL title 3 times. The team of Chennai Super Kings has played the most number of final matches eight times in the history of IPL which is a record in itself.Dhoni is not MS Dhoni for Chennai Cricket Lovers, he will be the Thala forever.
